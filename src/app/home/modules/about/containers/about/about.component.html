NG-BE 2019

NG-BE is the Belgian Angular conference and will be held at the beautiful Holiday Inn Ghent, Expo venue in Ghent on December 5 & 6, 2019.

Workshop day

December 5 is a multi-track workshop day, where attendees can learn about the latest Angular developments.

Conference day

December 6 is a single-track conference day. Attendees will be able to listen to talks from world-renowned Angular experts while enjoying delicious foods and drinks.
